# Asset attribution

All assets are real, downloaded works by human artists (none AI-generated).

## Code

- **Three.js** r169 (MIT) — https://github.com/mrdoob/three.js — `lib/`

## 3D models (`assets/models/`)

- **Xbot.glb**, **Soldier.glb** — Mixamo characters distributed with the
  three.js examples (https://github.com/mrdoob/three.js/tree/dev/examples/models/gltf)
- **RobotExpressive.glb** — by Tomás Laulhé (CC0), distributed with the
  three.js examples
- **city/** (buildings, trees, pavement) — Kenney *Starter Kit City Builder*
  (CC0) — https://github.com/KenneyNL/Starter-Kit-City-Builder
- **platformer/** (coin, flag, character) — Kenney *Starter Kit 3D Platformer*
  (CC0) — https://github.com/KenneyNL/Starter-Kit-3D-Platformer
- **fps/wall-high.glb** — Kenney *Starter Kit FPS* (CC0) —
  https://github.com/KenneyNL/Starter-Kit-FPS

## Audio (`assets/audio/`)

- **sfx/** blaster, lazer, explosion, death (alien_death1), shot, squit,
  pickup, click (key) — sound effects bundled with the Phaser 3 examples
  (https://github.com/photonstorm/phaser3-examples), free game audio packs
- **sfx/cannon.mp3** — BabylonJS assets (Apache-2.0) —
  https://github.com/BabylonJS/Assets
- **sfx/coin.ogg, thud1/thud2 (jump/land), fall.ogg** — Kenney *Starter Kit
  3D Platformer* sounds (CC0)
- **sfx/pong.mp3** — three.js examples audio
- **music/theme.mp3** — "358232_j_s_song" from the three.js example sounds
  (freesound.org sample #358232)
- **music/ambience.ogg** — Kenney *Starter Kit City Builder* (CC0)
